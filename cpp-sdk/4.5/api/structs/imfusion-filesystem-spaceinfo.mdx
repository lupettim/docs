`#include <ImFusion/Core/Filesystem/Path.h>`

## Overview

Simple structure representing the total and remaining disk space.

## Public Attributes

- [ByteSize](/cpp-sdk/4.5/api/classes/imfusion-bytesize) **capacity** — total disk space
- [ByteSize](/cpp-sdk/4.5/api/classes/imfusion-bytesize) **free** — total remaining disk space
- [ByteSize](/cpp-sdk/4.5/api/classes/imfusion-bytesize) **available** — available remaining disk space (may be less than *free* due to permissions)
