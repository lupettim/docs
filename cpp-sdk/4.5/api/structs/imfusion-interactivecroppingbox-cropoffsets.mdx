## Overview

Helper struct for storing the offsets of the lower and upper corner points of the box.

## Public Attributes

- vec3i **lower**
- vec3i **upper** — distance in pixels from the lower corner of the image to the lower corner of the [CropBox](/cpp-sdk/4.5/api/structs/imfusion-interactivecroppingbox-cropbox)
