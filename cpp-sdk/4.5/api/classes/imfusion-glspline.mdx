`#include <ImFusion/GL/GlSpline.h>`

## Overview

[Spline](/cpp-sdk/4.5/api/classes/imfusion-spline) annotation.

Renders a spline. Splines are parameterized in the range [0,1].

## Public Types

- enum class **RenderMode2d** &#123; **Line** , **Tube** , **TubeHollow** , **Xray** &#125;
- enum class **RenderMode3d** &#123; **Line** , **Tube** , **TubeHollow** , **Xray** &#125; — Enumeration to determine rendering order of GlObjects in [GlSliceView](/cpp-sdk/4.5/api/classes/imfusion-glsliceview) and [GlVolumeView](/cpp-sdk/4.5/api/classes/imfusion-glvolumeview).
