## Overview

Segment the skin surface from a CT or MRI volume.

The algorithm attempts to automatically segment the skin surface in a single surface as label map. It applies a thresholding step, connected component analysis, morphological filtering, and tries to remove enclosed air regions before composing the final label volume.
