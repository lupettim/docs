`#include <ImFusion/Reg/GatedLinear4DImageRegistration.h>`

## Overview

Registers two volume clips using a rigid/affine transformation, including a gating phase offset between the two.

Performs a non-linear optimization to find the maximum of a similarity measure that indicates an alignment of the two image sets.
