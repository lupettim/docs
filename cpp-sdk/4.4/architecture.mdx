The ImFusion C\+\+ SDK 4.4 follows a Model-View-Controller design.

## Core components

- **DataModel** — holds shared image data, annotations, and derived results.
- **DisplayWidgetMulti** — multi-view Qt widget rendering 2D/3D/4D data.
- **ApplicationController** — coordinates interaction, algorithms, and views.
- **Algorithm / BaseAlgorithm** — the unit of processing.
